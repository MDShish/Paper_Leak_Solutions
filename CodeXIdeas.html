Use of Generative-AI for Preventing Paper Leaks

Posted:July 5, 2024

Generative-AI vs Paper leaks: Generative AI, a branch of artificial intelligence that enables machines to generate content autonomously, holds immense potential in transforming how exam papers are created, stored, and distributed securely. Unlike traditional methods, where physical copies are vulnerable to theft or unauthorized access, Generative AI can generate unique sets of questions for each exam session dynamically. Paper leaks traditionally occur due to various vulnerabilities in the manual handling and distribution of physical exam papers. Despite stringent protocols, human error, insider threats, and logistical challenges often contribute to leaks. Once leaked, exam papers can be duplicated, shared, and accessed in advance, giving some candidates an unfair advantage over others.
